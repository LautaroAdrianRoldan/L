What is an IAT?

An IAT is a prompt model that draws primarily from your favorite things, such as music, people who are important to you, personalities, books, etc. It's a very simple prompt, yet incredibly powerful in its simplicity.

IAT = Tetrachromatic Artificial Intelligence
